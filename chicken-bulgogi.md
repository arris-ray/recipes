# Chicken Bulgogi

## Ingredients

- Chicken Breast (skinless, boneless) (~10)

- Green Onion (1 bunch)

- Pearl Onion (1 whole)

- Garlic (4 cloves)

- Ginger

## Cook

* Cut & mix all in a large mixing bowl

* Add ½ cup sugar in bowl

* Add ½ cup (or less) sesame oil

* Add ½ cup (or more) soy sauce

* Broil at 500 degrees for ~20 mins, each side
